Emotional Support

Resources, helplines, and tips for mental wellbeing.

When feeling overwhelmed

If you ever feel stressed or anxious, try grounding yourself by noticing your surroundings. Name five things you can see, four things you can touch, and three things you can hear. This simple practice helps you reconnect with the present moment and calm your mind.

Local helplines

Immediate help is available through local helplines. You can find them on our contact page or by searching online. Don’t hesitate to reach out — support is just a call or click away. search online .

Talk to someone

Sharing your feelings can make a big difference. Reach out to a friend, family member, or trusted person, or use online chat support platforms. Talking openly helps you process your emotions and gain perspective.(links are placeholders).
