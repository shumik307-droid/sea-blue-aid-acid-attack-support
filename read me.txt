# 🌊 SeaBlue Aid Website

SeaBlue Aid is a community-driven support platform designed to provide **medical, emotional, and financial assistance** to survivors.  
The website highlights awareness, resources, and opportunities to contribute via donations and volunteering.  

---

## 📌 Features
- **Responsive Navigation** — mobile-friendly menu toggle.
- **Statistics Section** — animated counters showing impact (survivors helped, volunteers, donations).
- **Stimulator Button** — dynamically increases statistics to demonstrate growth.
- **First Aid Information** — essential emergency guidance for survivors.
- **Donation Page** — easy access for financial support.
- **Emotional & Financial Support Pages** — connect survivors with the right resources.
- **About & Contact Pages** — transparency and communication.
- **Footer Newsletter Form** — subscribe to updates (demo version with JS alerts).
- **Contact/Other Forms** — functional demo handling (works without backend).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML5** — structure and content  
- **CSS3** — styling (responsive, card layout, button effects)  
- **JavaScript (ES6)** — interactivity (stats animation, forms, menu toggle)  
- **Fonts & Icons**: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) (Inter), [Font Awesome](https://fontawesome.com/)
